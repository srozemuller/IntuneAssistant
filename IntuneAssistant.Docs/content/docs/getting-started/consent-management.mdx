# Managing Admin Consent

When IntuneAssistant adds new features that require additional permissions, you may see a "Consent Required" notification.

## When Consent is Needed

You'll see **Consent Required** (1) at the top of your page when:
- New permissions have been added to IntuneAssistant
- Your tenant requires admin consent for the updated permissions

![Consent Required Banner](/img/getting-started/consent-required.png)

## Granting Consent

### Step 1: Navigate to Customer Page
Click the notification or go directly to the **Customer** page in your dashboard.

### Step 2: Locate Consent Status
Find your tenant in the list and look for **Consent Required** under the **Consent Status** column.

![Consent Required Banner](/img/getting-started/consent-required.png)

### Step 3: Grant Consent
1. Click the **Grant Consent** button next to your tenant (2)
2. A popup window will open with Microsoft's consent page
3. Review the new permissions being requested
4. Click **Accept** to grant the additional permissions


### Step 4: Verification
After successful consent, the status will update to **Granted** and you'll have access to the new features.
![Consent Granted](/img/getting-started/consent-granted.png)

## Troubleshooting

If consent fails or you encounter issues:
- Ensure you have Global Administrator rights in your tenant
- Check that popup blockers aren't preventing the consent window
- Try refreshing the page and attempting consent again
