# Onboarding

Welcome to **IntuneAssistant**! Thank you for choosing our tool to enhance your Microsoft Intune management experience. This guide will walk you through the onboarding process to get your tenant connected and ready to use all features.

<Callout type="info" title="What is onboarding?">
Onboarding connects your Microsoft tenant to IntuneAssistant by consenting our applications to access your Intune environment. This secure process enables the tool to retrieve and display your tenant data while maintaining strict privacy standards.
</Callout>

### Two Key Applications

During onboarding, two applications will be registered in your tenant:

1. **IntuneAssistant Application** - The main web interface you'll use
2. **IntuneAssistant API Application** - Handles secure data retrieval from your tenant

<Callout type="warn" title="Privacy Commitment">
**No tenant data is stored in our backend database.** Only your tenant ID and domain are registered for licensing and security purposes. All Intune data shown in the application exists only in your browser session and is removed when you log out.
</Callout>

## Onboarding Process

Getting started with IntuneAssistant is simple and takes just a few minutes:

<Steps>

### Navigate to Onboarding

Go to the [IntuneAssistant onboarding page](https://intuneassistant.cloud/onboarding) to begin the process.

![Onboarding Form](/img/getting-started/start-onboarding.png)

    Four steps are needed to complete onboarding:
    1. Provide Tenant Information
    2. Validate provided details
    3. Grant Consent
    4. Welcome to IntuneAssistant

### Provide Tenant Information

Enter your tenant details:
    - **Customer Name**: Your company or organization name
    - **Tenant ID**: Your Azure AD tenant identifier
    - **Tenant Domain**: Your organization's Microsoft 365 domain

    ![Onboarding Form](/img/getting-started/provide-details.png)

### Validate Details & Open Consent Page
    ![Onboarding Form](/img/getting-started/open-admin-consent.png)

Click the **Open consent** button. The system will verify your tenant details. If valid, you'll proceed to the consent page. A pop-up window will open for Microsoft login.
    After validation, you will see the following confirmation:
    ![Onboarding Form](/img/getting-started/onboarding-completed.png)
    Close the pop-up to return to IntuneAssistant.

### Welcome to IntuneAssistant
    At this point, your tenant is registered in our backend. You can now start using IntuneAssistant features.
    ![Onboarding Form](/img/getting-started/welcome-message.png)

### Ready to use IntuneAssistant

    After successful consent, you'll be redirected back to IntuneAssistant with your tenant fully configured and ready to use.
    Sign in (using sign in button below) with your organizational account to access the dashboard and start using IntuneAssistant.

## Post-Onboarding Verification

    Once onboarding is complete, you can verify the setup:
    You can verify your onboarding status on the customer page (2) using the user context menu (1) below
    ![Onboarding Form](/img/getting-started/verify-onboarding-status.png)

    The consent status (3) should show as **Ready**.

</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Security & Privacy">
    **No tenant data is stored in the backend database.** Only your tenant ID and domain are registered for licensing and security purposes. All Intune data shown in the application exists only in your browser session and is removed when you log out.
    For more details, please refer to the [Security page](https://intuneassistant.cloud/security/).
</Callout>
### New Applications in Your Tenant

Check your Azure AD App Registrations - you should see two new applications:


- **IntuneAssistant** - Main application for user authentication
- **IntuneAssistant API** - Backend service for data retrieval

## Troubleshooting

Having issues with onboarding? Check our troubleshooting guide:

- **Consent errors**: Ensure you have Global Administrator permissions
- **Application not visible**: Check Azure AD App Registrations after 5-10 minutes
- **Access denied**: Verify your account has appropriate Intune permissions
